Sai Group project

I created this website for my client.

During my freelancing journey, I had the opportunity to collaborate on an exciting project with Sai Group, a leading provider of cleaning services. The project involved developing a modern and user-centric website that would serve as the digital gateway to showcase Sai Group's comprehensive range of cleaning solutions. As the lead web developer on the project, I was entrusted with the pivotal role of translating the client's vision into reality. From conceptualization to implementation, I leveraged my expertise in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to craft an intuitive and aesthetically pleasing website. My responsibilities included designing the website's layout, implementing interactive features, and ensuring seamless functionality across different browsers and devices. In close partnership with the Sai Group team, I meticulously fine-tuned the website's design and functionality to ensure seamless navigation and optimal user experience. Integrating engaging visuals, relevant content, and clear calls-to-action, we aimed to convert website visitors into potential clients. Additionally, the website was built with a responsive design, adapting flawlessly to different screen sizes, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones, to reach a broader audience. Throughout the project's development, I maintained regular communication with Sai Group, providing progress updates and welcoming their feedback. This open and collaborative approach allowed us to make timely adjustments and implement any requested changes, resulting in a website that truly represented Sai Group's brand identity and values.

Technology Used:

HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap
